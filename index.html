Small gifts for big moments

Making children's eyes light up and putting a smile on people's faces - that is our mission! We love it when our creative young ones develop their creativity with our TOPModel sticker and colouring books, when Miss Melody boosts our horse enthusiasts' imagination, or when Dino World + ACTION gets curious children excited. We are also happy when our small gifts or tokens of affection make adults happy. Go on and find very special presents for family, friends or acquaintances - check out our diverse selection of brands and theme worlds!

About us

Depesche - Behind the scenes ...

You would like to know who is behind the creative toy brands like TOPModel, Miss Melody and Dino World? We let you take a peek behind the scenes and show you who is dreaming up the new collections on a yearly basis – and how it all started ...

Founded 1985 in Hamburg by Kjeld Schiøtz, Depesche now has more than 320 employees and is one of Europe's leading manufacturers of gifts and greeting cards.
